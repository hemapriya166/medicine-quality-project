About MedQuality

MedQuality

Online Testing and Monitoring of Quality of Medicines and Consumables

MedQuality is a comprehensive web-based quality monitoring system designed to digitize and automate the process of testing and monitoring the quality of medicines and consumables in healthcare settings. The system replaces traditional paper-based quality tracking with a real-time, cloud-connected platform accessible from anywhere.

🎯 Purpose

Problem

Manual paper-based quality tracking is slow, error-prone, and lacks real-time monitoring capabilities. There is no way for the general public to verify medicine quality.

Solution

MedQuality provides a digital platform for real-time quality testing, automatic pass/fail determination, instant alert generation, and a public medicine quality checker.

⚗️ Quality Parameters

Medicine quality parameters

Parameter Pass criteria Why it matters
Purity % ≥ 95% Ensures medicine is effective and safe
pH level 5.5 to 7.5 Affects absorption and stability
Moisture content % ≤ 2% Prevents degradation and microbial growth
Dissolution rate % ≥ 85% Ensures medicine dissolves properly in body
Appearance Good Visual inspection for physical defects

Consumable quality parameters

Parameter Pass criteria Why it matters
Condition Good Ensures consumable is usable
Sterility check Pass Prevents infection and contamination
Physical integrity Intact Ensures no damage or compromise
Expiry date Future date Expired consumables are unsafe to use
